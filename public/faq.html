General Questions

Q: What is UIU Connect?
A: UIU Connect is a community platform for students, faculty, and alumni of UIU to connect, share resources, and discuss topics related to academic and student life.

Q: How do I create an account?
A: You can sign up from the homepage (/) by providing your name, email, username, and a password.

Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
A: Currently, there is no "Forgot Password" feature. Please contact an administrator for assistance.

Forum Questions

Q: How do I post a new topic?
A: Navigate to the Forum page, select a section, and you will find an option to create a new topic within that section.

Q: What is the reputation system?
A: The reputation system allows users to gain points by contributing positively to the community (e.g., posting helpful content, receiving upvotes). It encourages good behavior and helps identify valuable members.

Q: Can I edit or delete my posts?
A: Yes, you can edit or delete your own IRC chat messages and forum posts shortly after you post them.. The original topic post cannot be deleted, only the entire topic.. Admins have permission to edit or delete any message.

Access Roles and Permissions

Q: What are the different user roles?
A: UIU Connect uses several roles to define user permissions:

  • Student: This is the default role for new users. Students can post in most forum sections, send private messages, and use all general features.
  • Faculty: Faculty members can post official announcements, post events, and post job opportunities. They can also manage (edit/delete/lock) their own forum topics and delete their own shared files.
  • Alumni: Alumni can create public profiles to connect with other alumni and students. They can also post job opportunities in the Career Corner.
  • Admin: This role has full administrative control over the platform. Admins can manage all users and roles, control forum section access, post and delete any announcement, and delete any forum topic.
